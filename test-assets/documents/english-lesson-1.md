# Lesson 1. Greetings and Introductions

**Course:** English for beginners
**Module:** Unit 1 — Meeting People
**Level:** A1
**Duration:** ~25 minutes

## Learning goals

By the end of this lesson you will be able to:
- greet people in different situations
- introduce yourself and others
- ask and answer basic personal questions

## Key phrases

| English | Русский | Transcription |
|---|---|---|
| Hello / Hi | Привет | /həˈləʊ/ /haɪ/ |
| Good morning | Доброе утро | /ɡʊd ˈmɔːnɪŋ/ |
| Good afternoon | Добрый день | /ɡʊd ˌɑːftəˈnuːn/ |
| Good evening | Добрый вечер | /ɡʊd ˈiːvnɪŋ/ |
| Nice to meet you | Приятно познакомиться | /naɪs tə miːt juː/ |
| My name is… | Меня зовут… | /maɪ neɪm ɪz/ |
| What's your name? | Как тебя зовут? | /wɒts jɔː neɪm/ |
| How are you? | Как дела? | /haʊ ɑː juː/ |
| I'm fine, thank you | Спасибо, хорошо | /aɪm faɪn θæŋk juː/ |

## Grammar focus: verb "to be"

| Pronoun | Form | Example |
|---|---|---|
| I | am | I **am** a student. |
| You | are | You **are** kind. |
| He / She / It | is | She **is** a teacher. |
| We / You / They | are | They **are** friends. |

## Dialogue

> **Anna:** Hi! My name is Anna. What's your name?
> **Ben:** Hello, Anna. I'm Ben. Nice to meet you.
> **Anna:** Nice to meet you too. Where are you from?
> **Ben:** I'm from London. And you?
> **Anna:** I'm from Moscow.

## Exercise

Fill in the gaps with *am / is / are*:

1. I ___ a student.
2. She ___ my sister.
3. We ___ friends.
4. They ___ from Italy.
5. He ___ a teacher.

*(Answers: 1. am, 2. is, 3. are, 4. are, 5. is)*
